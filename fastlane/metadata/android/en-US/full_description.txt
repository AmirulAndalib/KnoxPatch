<p><i>KnoxPatch</i> is a LSPosed module to get Samsung apps/features working again in your rooted Galaxy device. It is accompanied by KnoxPatch Enhancer (available via the <a href='https://apt.izzysoft.de/magisk'>IzzyOnDroid Magisk Module Repository</a>), a Magisk module that will take care of the non-fixable apps/features via the Xposed API's, such as the Galaxy Wearable apps.</p>
